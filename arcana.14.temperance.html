Temperance

Upright

Balance, Patience, Strategy

  1. Use tact and strategy to build the foundation then climb to your goals.
  2. Look for and live in situations free from stress.
  3. Patience and moderation are keys to forward moves. Remain calm!
  4. Take the high road over all external circumstance.
  5. Choose optimism. All perspectives should be accommodated.
  6. Just a moment's visualization can put you right on course. Give your higher being time to see what's needed.

 

Reversed

Imbalance, Discord, Frustration

Life can feel off balance, leading to anxiety and stress. Prioritize your activities (passions) to find more joy. Ask for help! Schedule the small steps. Seek incrementalism. Don't get overwhelmed by the big picture. Restore balance and moderation immediately. Lots of energy around, and not much of it is positive. You may have to engage in hard conversations to help clear the air. Combine spontaneity with knowledge. Consider what is appropriate in a situation. Let old habits and old fears die!
