Intuition Guidebook

I began writing my own guidebook in March of 2020. I took a blank journal with 160 pages and wrote the card names across the top. I finished one and have started others. Eventually, I was encouraged to put the whole thing online to help others do the same.

These card meanings are the statements and questions I ask myself when referencing the matching tarot card. I have no connection to anything supernatural with tarot cards. I read the meanings of the cards and write myself challenging thoughts based on the ancient meanings. Apply at your own risk. :)

If you see a card that has 6 sayings or lessons, the idea is to get a 6-sided die and roll it to see which item you can focus on during the day.

I can still be reached via email at tarot@davidj.org.

If you'd like to start your own guidebook, I'd be honored if you used mine as a reference.
