King of Pentacles

Upright

Attainment, Prosperity, Abundance

Seek quality over quantity to raise awareness of all things. Translate your vision into something tangible. There are many places you have already proven yourself. Work hard, methodically and conscientously. A well-thought-out plan will lead to success. Be pragmatic and stay informed. Be generous and courageous. Enjoy the natural world around you as the comfort of your wealth.

 

 

 

Reversed

Authorative, Controlling, Domineering

Are you showing an excessive interest in material pleasures? (include food!)? Are you focusing on negative aspects of money? It is time to lighten up on yourself and others. Get some air.
