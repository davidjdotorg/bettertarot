The Hermit

Upright

Soul Searching, Reflection, Truth

Seeking truth tends to be a solo journey. You define your core values. Align your body and mind. Consciousness desires to move inward; Let it! Honor the wisdom within yourself. What do I have that is only mine?

  • Realing your mind's potential thru introspection and contemplation gathers self-confidence.

 

 

Reversed

Loneliness, Confinement, Withdraw

  1. Have you heard from your calm self lately or is your angry self running the narrative?
  2. Rebuild yourself from your right self. Let that self dictate the time you spend alone and how you spend it.
  3. Connecting with a higher self can make you feel less alone. That higher self could simply be the best version of you. Find it!
  4. Bring yourself to a state of non-being, then listen to what you have to say.
  5. If you find you have something to share, do it gently.
  6. Freedom comes thru working to achieve inner perfection. Find the teacher within. Let the goal become the source.
