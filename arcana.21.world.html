The World

Upright

Completion, Integration, Accomplishment

  1. Find an acceptance of yourself and others that creates unity.
  2. Define your role in the systems at play around you. Choose to change or champion that role with your highest effort.
  3. Transcend possessions and desires by facing the death of all that flows thru you.
  4. Bring fullness into the present moment, then base your actions accordingly.
  5. Walking new paths shines light on the differences from the old paths and can bring the fulfillment and vision you seek.
  6. You are of the world. Your entire body grew from physical material. Act like it.
