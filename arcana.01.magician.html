The Magician

Upright

Inventive, Manifestation, Potential

  1. You have the knowledge you need. Now use your skills to spot misgivings.
  2. You have creative power and energy to create a new life cycle for yourself.
  3. Feel that strong sense of optimism. Apply each initiative with skill.
  4. You will provide solutions. You will use your existing knowledge and networks to clear a path.
  5. The universe is impartial. It is your energy that matters most. Go for it!
  6. Take stock of your resources. Accentuate the positive.

 

Reversed

Trickery, Manipulation, Potential

Clarity is sought. Vulnerability makes you a target for manipulation. Where are you focusing your energy? Are your objectives clear? What skill can you apply? Be authentic! What change can make you a better person? Are you spiritual? Can you allow your intuition to let yourself feel spiritual? Believing in yourself means presenting yourself as a believer in you!
