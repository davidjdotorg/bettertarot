Knight of Cups

Upright

Romantic, Emotional Elation, Adoration

In tune with intuition, ready for deep conversations, seek out high-spiritedness! Wear that heart on that sleeve!

Express yourself openly, you have the emotional support you need. Be open to receive.

How will you respond to important news? It's okay to move slow and enjoy the ride as a creative process. This is not about a finish line, but a journey. If you are making a decision, consider your feelings.

Reversed

Unrealistic, Jealous, Moody

Don't let your emotions take you over. Avoid making assumptions or jumping to conclusions due to your emotions.

A good way to begin separating yourself from your emotions (to keep them from controlling you) is to do an emotional check-in. Throughout the day, take a moment and relax your body. Focus on what your emotions are telling you. What sensations are you feeling?

Are you looking at things you've imagined into your reality or reality itself? Reality should not be skewed by your emotions. Ask yourself if something is the way it is or am I adding meaning to it?

What really belongs to you? Does reality belong to you? Does your imagined reality belong to you? Let it all go, man!

If you feel yourself getting angry, take a moment and breathe. Exhale all that stuff!
