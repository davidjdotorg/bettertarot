Two of Cups

Upright

Partnership, Complimentary, Duality

  1. Poella. Wife. Trust and mutual respect. Your commitment has been built over time.
  2. Are you expecting reciprocation?
  3. Be respectful in all conversations.
  4. To be truly loved, deeply treasured, and highly valued by others, first create those feelings for yourself. Then respect them in you by finding them in others.
  5. Reconciliation, harmony, and an end to struggles is possible.
  6. - (got a suggestion?)

Reversed

Clash, Disconnection

Are you seeing eye to eye with your partner? Don't blame the past. Check whether you have unresolved patterns that play in. Are you accepting yourself in love? Open up. Share. Create or broaden your safe space.
