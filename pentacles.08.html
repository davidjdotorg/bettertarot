Eight of Pentacles

Upright

Hustle, Grit, Craftsmanship

Learning by doing is exactly what you're up to right now. Be completely focused on absorbing new skills and mastering your craft. Are you applying yourself? Are you committed with focused attention? Bring your whole self on each step of your journey. Be willing to work hard and pay attention to details. Whatever your hands find to do, do it with all your might! Be committed to change and progress. Make adjustments as you go. Energy invested in your personal development will not be wasted. Focusing on the right things brings peace.

Reversed

Perfectionism, Lack of Focus

What if you were called on to do something completely different? Are you seeing your desired outcomes? Are you working towards the right things? The time is right to change direction. Feeling impatient can lead to envy and frustration. Are you focusing on(success or results) instead of the work to get there?

  • Diligent effort is the pathway to displaying your talent.
  • The satisfaction of cleaning the kitchen is in having a clean kitchen!
  • "Is he sick?" "No, it's worse... he's discouraged.
