Nine of Pentacles

Upright

True Colors, Splendor, Vitality

Indulge yourself! You feel at ease with life. Live loud and enjoy your creation. Staying true to yourself improves your quality of life. Connect with and replenish your uniqueness. Maintain reality in your environment. Focus your awareness on what you have. Doing so will show you are productive and alive! You have the ability to know things others can only guess.

 

 

 

Reversed

Self-Sabotage, Materialistic Dependency

  1. When you know you were menat for more, review your core beliefs and change your mindset as required.
  2. A lack of discipline, routine, rituals or whatever is lacking should be corrected now.
  3. Do you have abandoned projects or issues that need revisited? Seek useful purposes and channel energy directly towards them.
  4. Oftentimes it is a setback that reveals true desires. Aim for self-awareness.
  5. Are you working too hard to have the best of everything? Focus on family and friends time and not on how you provide.
  6. Reduce your dependence on material wealth. Grow what makes you feel rich: relations.
