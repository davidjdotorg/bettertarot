Queen of Pentacles

Positive

Nourishment, Earth Mother, Hearth

You are working to have, so be ready to share. Experience love, comfort, and support. Be practical and resourceful in your life. Make a little go a long way. Maintain a compassionate, nurturing, practical and down-to-earth attitude when dealing with others and your present circumstances. Find time for yourself and prioritize 'me' time in between all your other commitments. Be absolutely devoted to the people you most care about. Share your wisdom with gentle kindness but do share it. You are capable of guiding others by what you have learned.

Negative

Working, Smothering, Isolation

Take a step back and see where you can spend less time in control. Can you sustain your own lifestyle? Could you rebuild in two weeks time? It is easier to take care of others when you are taking care of yourself. Release the desire to be in command. Allow more time for thoughtful solitude. Sit. Nothing else.
