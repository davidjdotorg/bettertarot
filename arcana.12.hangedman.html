The Hanged Man

Positive

Sacrifice, Patience, Letting go

Hold on! This may be time to delay a decision. Take time to think thru other options. Is there something you can put down to help you reach your goal? When you accept and surrender to your present circumstances you get a view from a new perspective. Honor the system over the goal. Give up your attachment to results. Make the process of the highest importance. When you practice patience, other opportunities may unfold. Do not care so much whether you are understood by others. Instead, seek to understand.

Negative

Avoidance, Delay, Indecision

Stop sacrificing your time and values just to please other people. Your life is bigger than this moment. Pause and get perspective. Surrender to what is and let go of what should be. Solidify the mindset you most enjoy. Find the space where you can tune in. Remain in reality while rooted in your passions. What is moving fast? Your body, mind or life?

The path to enlightenment is often a painful one.

Sometimes the only way out is through.

There is great wisdom in sacrifice; there is beauty in surrender.

True freedom comes when we release our attachments to outcomes.

Letting go of control is the ultimate act of trust.

Accepting what is is the first step to a transformation.

We must be willing to release old beliefs to make space for new growth.

Be willing to see things from a new perspective.
