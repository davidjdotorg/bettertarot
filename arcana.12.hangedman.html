The Hanged Man

Upright

Sacrifice, Patience, Letting go

  1. Hold on! This may be time to delay a decision. Take time to think thru other options.
  2. Is there something you can put down to help you reach your goal?
  3. When you accept and surrender to your present circumstances you get a view from a new perspective.
  4. Honor the system over the goal. Give up your attachment to results. Make the process of the highest importance.
  5. When you practice patience, other opportunities may unfold.
  6. Do not care so much whether you are understood by others. Instead, seek to understand.

 

 

Reversed

Avoidance, Delay, Indecision

  1. Stop sacrificing your time and values just to please other people.
  2. Your life is bigger than this moment. Pause and get perspective.
  3. Surrender to what is and let go of what should be.
  4. Solidify the mindset you most enjoy. Find the space where you can tune in.
  5. Remain in reality while rooted in your passions.
  6. What is moving fast? Your body, mind or life?
