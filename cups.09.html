Nine of Cups

Positive

Wish Come True, Fulfillment, Pleasures

Your ideal lifestyle comes to fruition. Everything is in balance. Now balance big thoughts with small actions. You have paved your own way. Live in the moment. Don't spoil what you have by desiring what you have not.

Negative

Greed, Materialism

Be aware of unrealistic expectations.

Apply moderation in all you do.

Are you sure your intentions are resolved to the highest good? Be sure you are pursuing that which is in your best interests and the best interests of those you care about.

You must be settled with your definition of success. And also understand when you are not working towards the greater good.
