The Chariot

Positive

Victory, Willpower, Inner Drive

Hands on the steering wheel, be ready to go! Your willpower is driving you towards your destiny. Challenges will make you stronger. Maintain Focus! Run your own race and ignore the haters. Leverage opposing forces to your advantage. Be determined, self-disciplined and hard-working. Maintain focus, determination, and confidence in your own abilities.

Negative

Directionless, Off Course, Roaming

Sometimes life can 'run you over' with so much drama you find it hard to keep up. Watch out for road rage! If you can feel you could go from zero to one hundred, put the conversation on pause. Get outside and let a destinationless walk remind you how much the journey matters. If there's something you can't control, ride it out without worry or blame or guilt. Focus on the thing that matters most (usually love!). Make sure your attitude and energy aligns with your passions and your boundaries. Stop claiming power you do not have; Build what you need!

  • We all float on!

Though your friends and family may have opposing interests, you know what works for you. Treat all with respect and appreciation.

Tolerance and acceptance are not indicators of approval. Compromise often leads to a greater good.

You have agency! Take the driver's seat when able.

Dress yourself in your beliefs. Embrace those symbols closest to you.

How do you move a vehicle without a steering wheel or a horse with no reins?
