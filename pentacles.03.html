Three of Pentacles

Upright

Teamwork, Collaboration, Synergy

  1. Avoid any sense of superiority and focus on your role. Whether family, worker or helper, do your best.
  2. Seek collaboration. There are great people around you with a strong desire to help. You also show these qualities.
  3. Recognizing synergy means seeing how you have actied to find it so you can maintain it.
  4. Gather! Create space for gathering. Require the honoring of that space as a diverse gathering spot. Invite!
  5. When you are part of something bigger your talents and your needs are magnified.
  6. Show appreciation for the unique qualities in others.

 

Reversed

Challenge, Power Struggle, Competition

  1. Competition drains time away from working together.
  2. Avoid blaming. Be the person that talks up their counterparts and strives for better.
  3. Release the resentments of past slights and focus only on your behavior.
  4. Never feign knowledge. Bring more planning and organization to your environment.
  5. Make sure all voices are heard. Set clear guidelines for all involved to keep all involved.
  6. Each person's contribution comes from their life experience making it unique, and making it matter.
