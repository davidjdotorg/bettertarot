The Devil

Positive

Addiction, Enslavement, Fears

Watch for concerning negative behaviors and patterns that restrain you from moving forward. Are you hiding yourself from something? What are the addictions or attachments restraining you from experiencing freedom? How am I giving away my power? Are other people or other things dictating my actions or decisions? Do not let fear or insecurity hold you back! What is the balance between spiritual and material things in my life? Transform negative energy into positive growth.

Negative

Awareness, Break Free, Empowerment

Be open to different ways of living. Eliminate negativity. Watch out for temptations. You may be about to level up. This is a call to higher potential. Deal with your shadows while you can. Dare to understand. Review your behaviors while asking what is harmful to your inner psyche. It could be simple standards like healthier eating, better activities, or more focus. Remain faithful to your goal of authentic freedom. What attachments can you drop? If you consider your situation and respond thoughtfully, you can not do wrong.

It's not hard to love people because of who they are; It's hard to love people because of who we are.

Stay connected, stay current, stay calm.

The things you own end up owning you.

The only real prison is fear. The only real freedom is freedom from fear.

Nobody can hurt me without my permission.

You must not fool yourself, and you are the easiest person to fool.

It is never too late to be what you might have been.

One does not become enlightened by imagining figures of light, but by making the darkness conscious.

The first step towards change is awareness. The second step is acceptance.
